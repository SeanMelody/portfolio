About Me

Hi! My name is Sean Melody, and I am learning to be a full stack web developer. I have a Bachelor's degree in Photography and a Minor in Art History. I am a UC Berkeley extension educated MERN developer with an extensive knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Node, Express, mySql and Handlebars, with many more still to come.

I enjoy creating, designing and helping to bring ideas and visions to life on both the front and back end of webpages. Forever optimistic, with a strong work ethic, love problem solving/debugging and very detail oriented. I consider myself a master of deadlines and crave the excitement of solving complex problems and learning new ways to do so. Team-oriented, company loyal and always happy to help anywhere it is needed as well as having the confidence to attack problems solo. Passionate about all things, but a few personal favorites are the SF Giants, travel and cats.
